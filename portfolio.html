Portfolio

Here are some of the projects I have worked on.

Longevity Diet is a Virginia-based single-page application designed and implemented for a bio-medical entrepreneur. The application offers customized holistic consultation subscriptions to people interested in healing natural remedies.

Donation pages for e-solicitation: One of the primary missions of the organization is to provide public media stations with tools to enable their audience (both members and prospects) to donate in a secure and efficient webpage. The landing page reflects the aesthetic values of the individual stations as well as seamlessly integrates with third party services. The header image and UI elements can be customized to reflect seasonal and special drives.

Landing page mock-up for USAlliance: As a part of the digital marketing initiative landing pages were needed for email campaigns. The design underlines the brand's forward-looking and features savings calculator widget. The placement of CTA/functionality is optimized for catching the interest of the visitors, ensuring a high conversion rate for the campaign.

Printable Program Schedule: This application generates a program schedule, based on available data. The challenge lay in mapping time blocks to divs representing sessions within a day. The container is optimized to fit within the standard browser size to minimize scrolling. The application also provides printable schedules for the day or week.
